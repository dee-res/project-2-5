E-Commerce

An e-commerce website is an online store where customers can buy products and services.

Business

A business website is a digital platform that allows a business to connect with potential customers and clients online.


Informational

An informational website is a website that provides information and resources to visitors about a specific topic, subject, or event.

Brochure

A brochure website is a digital version of a company's printed brochure that provides basic information about the business and its products or services.
